A report for Alex

What three.js
can actually do.

A handful of small, self-contained experiments — each one a probe into a different corner of the engine. Moody lighting, procedural motion, Gaussian splats, aerial LiDAR point clouds. All running live in the browser.

Experiments

—
  • 01

    Floating Monolith

    A slow-turning obelisk under moody lighting. Starter scene — proves the whole stack works end to end.

    →
  • 02

    Particle Drift

    A few thousand softly-glowing motes on lazy orbits in dark cinematic space. Warm terracotta cores, cool rim, subtle twinkle.

    →
  • 03

    Card Fan

    Five cards held by an invisible hand — drifting in, breathing on the spot, lifting at the touch of a cursor.

    →
  • 04

    Gaussian Splats

    Rendering photographs, volumetrically. A radiance field floats in the dark while the camera takes a slow, cinematic lap.

    →
  • 05

    LiDAR Scan

    Millions of laser returns from an aerial pass — a landscape drawn entirely in dust motes.
